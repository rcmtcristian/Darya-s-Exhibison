Darya Eremina

Photography is an art that seeks to reinterpret reality and transform it into images.

Magna feugiat sed adipiscing

Photographing is a public exhibition of the interior of oneself, a voluntary loss of intimacy, desires, tastes, fantasies, ideas...
Everything is recorded in photography, even the reflection of the soul! Photography beyond its beauty makes us question ourselves and ask ourselves a series of questions. If it does not succeed, it will only be one more beautiful image, which will be relegated to being only observed and forgotten, like some of the images that prevail in this world whose democratization of photography has created a society accustomed to seeing, but not to paying attention to the images that are around us.
